# Voice Principles
- Lowercase. Direct. No corporate fluff.
- Arrow bullets ( → ) for sequences.
- ALL CAPS for one or two emphasis words per post, never more.
- Specific numbers beat vague claims.
- End on a single-word CTA when possible.

# Structure
HOOK (one line) → SETUP (one line) → 3-5 ARROW BULLETS → CTA (single word)

# Signature Patterns
- "i used to X. now i Y."
- "→ here's how it actually works:"
- specific dollar amounts ("$12k/mo", "$847")
- timestamps ("in 10 minutes", "by 9am")

# What NOT to Do
- LinkedIn-bro hooks ("Most people don't realize...")
- Generic advice ("be authentic")
- Em dashes
- Title Case headlines

# Length / Format
- X: 280 chars max
- LinkedIn: 1200 chars body
- IG/Threads: 2200 chars body
