# Slide Arc

The 6-slide carousel structure used by branded-carousel.

## Roles

### Slide 1: HOOK
**Job:** Stop the scroll.
**Content:** One pattern-interrupt headline. No subtext. No icons.
**Example:** "I WORK 24 HOURS A DAY"
**Visual:** Largest typography of the deck. Massive negative space.

### Slide 2: REVEAL
**Job:** Resolve the hook with the twist.
**Content:** The reframe in two lines (or one + subtitle).
**Example:** "NOT ME. MY AGENTS."
**Visual:** Headline + secondary line, possibly a relevant icon.

### Slide 3: SETUP
**Job:** Bridge to value. Tell the reader what's coming.
**Content:** Transitional headline + teasing subtext.
**Example:** "WHAT THEY DO WHILE I SLEEP:"
**Visual:** Headline + small subtitle. Optional thematic icon.

### Slides 4–N: EXAMPLES (typically 2-3 of these)
**Job:** Concrete, ICP-specific wins.
**Content:** Numbered headline + 3-5 short bullet fragments.
**Example:** `1. CLIENT INBOX` + bullets [triages incoming, flags fires, drafts responses, escalates real urgent]
**Visual:** Large number badge in accent color, headline in primary, bullets in body weight.

### Penultimate: OUTCOME
**Job:** Emotional payoff. The "I wake up with..." moment.
**Content:** Bold outcome statement, one supporting line.
**Example:** "I WAKE UP WITH LEVERAGE."
**Visual:** Headline with KEY WORD highlighted in secondary accent color (e.g. yellow on dark). Sunrise / upward visual cue optional.

### Final: CTA
**Job:** Convert attention. Save + DM hook is the standard.
**Content:** One-line action + optional DM keyword.
**Example:** "BUILD YOUR 24/7 TEAM. comment AGENTS."
**Visual:** Headline in primary, prominent SAVE / button shape in accent.

## Counting examples

If the script provides 2 EXAMPLES, the carousel is 7 slides total. If 3, it's 8. Default is 2-3 examples = 6-7 slides. Cap at 10 total slides.

## Anti-patterns (drop on sight)

- "Slide X of Y" meta text
- Subtext duplicating the headline
- Icons on every slide ("decoration creep")
- Same layout repeated 3+ times in a row
- Bullets longer than 5 words on EXAMPLES slides
