# Pattern: dated_personal_anecdote

## Shape
Open with a specific date or dated scene. Walk through what happened. Land
on a generalized takeaway.

## Hook style
scene. Dated, sensory, specific.

## Format hint
narrative or process_reveal.

## Body density markers
- ISO or named dates
- counts (12 carousels, 5 marketers, 23 agents)
- specific people, places, tools
- a punchline beat that surprises

## Close style
save (homework) or share.

## Why it saves
Dated specifics signal "this actually happened to a real operator". The
generalization at the end gives the reader something to apply.

## Source exemplars from matt corpus
- "nov 20 2025. opus 4.5 shipped with skills."
- "10 minutes scrolling. 12 carousels."

## Bug 9 guard
Dated personal anecdotes claim personal facts. The skill MUST elicit operator
specifics before drafting. claims_personal_fact must be true on any concept
using this pattern.
