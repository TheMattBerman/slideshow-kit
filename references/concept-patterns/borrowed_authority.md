# Pattern: borrowed_authority

## Shape
Open with a quote from a known operator/founder. Contextualize. Layer your
take. Close with a comment-CTA.

## Hook style
dialogue. Quoted line is the load-bearing element.

## Format hint
receipt_context or counter_narrative.

## Body density markers
- specific quote (verbatim)
- speaker named with credibility (ARR, role, recent action)
- date of the quote
- your contextualization in your voice

## Close style
comment (DM the analysis) or share.

## Why it saves
Borrowed authority lifts you onto someone else's credibility for the price
of a quote. The reader saves to remember the quote AND your take.

## Source exemplars from matt corpus
- "lemkin said it on his last podcast..."
- "a $15M ARR founder told me..."
