# Pattern: stack_contrast

## Shape
Old-stack-cost vs new-stack-cost. Anchor a dollar amount or seat count to the
old way; juxtapose with the AI-native way.

## Hook style
single_claim. Specific dollar amount or scale claim.

## Format hint
narrative or before_after.

## Body density markers
- specific dollar amounts ($11k, $40k)
- specific tools (gpt image 2, claude, openclaw)
- before/after metrics (CTR, CPL, hours-to-output)

## Close style
save (audit your own stack) or share.

## Why it saves
Operators bookmark cost-comparisons to revisit when they're sizing their own
stack. The specific dollar amounts make the claim debatable AND quotable.

## Source exemplars from matt corpus
- "i replaced my $11k product photographer with one prompt"
- "gpt image 2 just killed my $11k product photographer"
- "i run my meta ads with @openclaw for $0/month"
