# Pattern: insider_observation

## Shape
Open by pointing at something the reader sees but hasn't named. Body slides
make the unnamed thing concrete. Close gives a way to act on the new awareness.

## Hook style
observation.

## Format hint
numbered_diagnostic or counter_narrative.

## Body density markers
- counts of the observed phenomenon
- specific examples the reader can verify
- a named framework for the phenomenon (often pairs with named_framework_close)

## Close style
save.

## Why it saves
Once-you-see-it-you-can't-unsee-it patterns become permanent mental tools.
Operators bookmark them as audit primitives.

## Source exemplars from matt corpus
- "scroll your feed. count the carousels with exactly 6 slides."
- "most founders are still hiring marketing teams"
