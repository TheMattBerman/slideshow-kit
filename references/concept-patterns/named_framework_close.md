# Pattern: named_framework_close

## Shape
Body slides build toward a 2-3 word framework name in the second-to-last or
close slide. The framework becomes the quotable line readers carry into
their next conversation.

## Hook style
any. Pattern is body-and-close-driven, not hook-driven.

## Format hint
numbered_diagnostic or anatomy_breakdown.

## Body density markers
- numbered tells / rules / steps
- a 2-3 word phrase introduced explicitly: "i call this the X"
- the phrase is repeated in the close

## Close style
save. Operator runs the named framework as homework.

## Why it saves
The named framework is the bookmark trigger. Without a name, body slides are
just paragraphs. With a name, the carousel becomes a recurring tool.

## Source exemplars from matt corpus
- "the 4 tells of every ai carousel" (tells = framework name)
- "the audit move" (audit = framework name)
- "the receipt rule" (receipt rule = framework name)
