# Pattern: agency_anchor

## Shape
Reference an agency invoice line item or retainer as the receipt; structure
the carousel around what replaces that line item.

## Hook style
scene or single_claim. Specific dated or dollar-amount opener.

## Format hint
narrative or process_reveal.

## Body density markers
- specific retainer amounts
- workflow steps
- date anchors (last quarter, march, tuesday)
- replacement tooling named (claude cowork, openclaw)

## Close style
save or comment (DM the workflow).

## Why it saves
Founders recognize their own agency invoices. The replacement workflow gets
saved for "when we do this next quarter".

## Source exemplars from matt corpus
- "tuesday 2pm. the agency invoice line was gone."
- "we replaced our $44k/quarter creative agency"
